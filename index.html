About the Hackathon

The BioML Hackathon brings together students, researchers, and developers to build machine learning tools that help us understand biology — from cells to molecules. Whether you're into gene expression, protein modeling, or drug discovery, there’s a challenge for you.

Challenge Tracks

  • 🔬 Predictive Genomics: Identify gene impact, simulate knockouts, or analyze scRNA-seq data
  • 🧠 Interpretable Biology: Build tools that help biologists visualize and understand ML outputs
  • 💊 Drug Discovery: Predict drug-target interactions or protein–ligand binding affinities (CASP-inspired)
  • 📊 Omics Data Science: Clean, analyze, and extract insights from complex multi-omics datasets

What to Expect

  • 💡 Real datasets from active research labs
  • 🎓 Mentorship from experts in biology and ML
  • 🏆 Prizes for innovation, impact, and usability
  • 🌍 Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams
  • 🚀 Fast-paced environment with room to publish or develop further
